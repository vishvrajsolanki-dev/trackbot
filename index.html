Future Development
Ideas Board

Features, upgrades, and research directions tracked for TrackBot 2 and beyond. Add your own ideas by copying an idea card in the HTML.

Planned — TrackBot 2
Scissor Fork Lift Mechanism
Expert-fabricated aluminium scissor lift. True vertical linear motion. NEMA 17 stepper. 500g payload capacity.
Planned — TrackBot 2
Autonomous Charging Dock
Robot navigates to charging station when battery drops below 15%. Fully autonomous operation without manual recharge.
Planned — TrackBot 2
Computer Vision Sorting
OpenMV H7 classifies payload type by colour or shape. Routes different payloads to different drop zones automatically.
Planned — Research
Multi-Robot Fleet Coordination
MQTT broker manages multiple TrackBot units. Central task scheduler with shared occupancy grid for collision avoidance.
Planned — Research
ArUco Marker Navigation
Replace RFID tags with ArUco visual markers. Camera reads markers for position correction. No physical tag hardware in arena.
Planned — Research
PID Auto-Tuning
Ziegler-Nichols relay feedback auto-tuning for all 4 PID controllers. Eliminates manual tuning. Better performance on varied surfaces.
